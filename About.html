School history

Exploring back the water of time, usuludeen-islammy Arabic and Islaimic school owed her existence to the year 1992 C.E as it hailed from her temporary site Galadima Compound Gegele, ilorin, where it flourish for four consecutive years after her founder (Alh Ahmad Abdulrahman Sardauna) set out in quest for contination of his university degree in Sokoto state between 1996-2000

In year 2003, the founder tried refurbishing the school at his new resident but all effort are aborted, He start teaching his own children and his best friend's (late Alh Yusuf Ayyuha-Sidiq) children from time range of 6am-7am for more than 7years

In 2010, the Surfaced and twinkled gilttering on the skyline of its permant site in Gbagba area, off Airport Road

Objective of the school
Among the outstanding objectives o the school is striving to achieve right from inception is training generations of studenton Acedemics per excellence on the platform of good upbringing to make them worthy leader of emulation in the nearest future.
